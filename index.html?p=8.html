FAQ

What happens if the plane/train is delayed?

We will track your flight/train online so it doesn’t matter if you are 1 hour or 10 hours delayed a driver will still be there to meet you at no extra charge.

Where do we meet the driver and how do we recognize him?

Airport transfers

Your driver will go to the arrivals hall and start to wait approx 20 minutes after the true landing time of your plane, he / she will have a sign with your name on it. There is a specific meeting point at both of the terminals.

Terminal 1 – when you come into the arrival hall if you look to your left you will see a BIG HOLIDAY INN SIGN, your driver will be standing in the direction off this

Terminal 2 – when you walk into the arrival hall if you look to your right you will see a TRAVELEX EXCHANGE OFFICE your driver will be standing in the direction of this.

If you don’t see your driver please stay inside the arrivals hall at the meeting point, wait 5-10 minutes and then call Neil on +420 773 179 630 because your driver won’t be far away.

If you have arrived but there is a delay / problem with luggage, please sms / call us.

Station transfers

If you don’t see your driver please wait at the meeting point, wait 5-10 minutes and then call Neil on +420 773 179 630 because your driver won’t be far away.

Hlavni Nadrazi / main station – When you arrive to Hlavni Nadrazi look for Raiffeisen bank in the station. Your driver will meet you in front of there, they will be holding a sign with your name on.

Nadrazi Holesovice – Please stay on the platform, your driver will meet you there with a sigh with your name on.

Do you supply baby seat?

Yes supplied F.O.C, just let us know the age of your child and if you require a car seat or booster seat on the booking form. Children under 150cm and weighing less than 36kg must have a child seat so by law.

Are your cars air-conditioned?

Yes our cars have air conditioning.

What currency can I pay in?

We prefer the local currency Czech crowns but we do accept EUR, GBP and USD, the price may vary slightly if paying in any currency other than Czech crowns.
